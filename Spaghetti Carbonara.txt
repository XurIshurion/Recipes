Spaghetti Carbonara
Ingredients:

1 pound spaghetti
4 large eggs
1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
4 ounces guanciale or pancetta, diced
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
Instructions:

Cook the spaghetti according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, Parmesan cheese, and pepper.
In a skillet, cook the guanciale or pancetta until crispy.
Toss the cooked spaghetti with the guanciale or pancetta.
Immediately add the egg mixture to the pasta and toss quickly until the eggs are scrambled and the pasta is coated.
Serve immediately.
