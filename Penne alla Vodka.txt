Penne alla Vodka
Ingredients:

1 pound penne pasta
1/2 cup vodka
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup diced pancetta or bacon
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Cook the penne pasta according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
In a large skillet, cook the pancetta or bacon until crispy. Remove from the skillet and set aside. Â  
Add the garlic to the skillet and cook for 30 seconds, or until fragrant.
Stir in the vodka and bring to a simmer. Cook for 1 minute, or until the alcohol has evaporated.
Add the heavy cream and Parmesan cheese, and simmer for 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
Toss the cooked pasta with the sauce and bacon. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
